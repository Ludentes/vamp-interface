## A day in her life

- **Shares things that make her look smart or funny**, not things that make her look worried.

## Her actual job-hunting workflow
1. Opens a job Telegram channel (2-3 she knows)
2. Scrolls fast, skips anything with "guaranteed income" or "work from home unlimited"
3. If the salary looks real, clicks → reads the description → looks for red flags she learned from the MLM experience ("no company name", "DM only", "no fixed schedule")
4. Maybe googles the company
5. Applies to 1-2 max per session. Loses motivation after 20 minutes.

She has never used a fraud scoring tool. She didn't know telejobs existed until Sonya told her.
